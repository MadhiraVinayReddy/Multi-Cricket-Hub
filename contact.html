Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take? ▾
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for metro cities at an additional charge.
What is the return policy? ▾
We offer a 7-day easy return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and returned with the invoice. Jerseys and customized items cannot be returned.
Are the IPL jerseys official? ▾
Yes! All IPL jerseys sold on CricketHub are official licensed products sourced directly from the BCCI and team merchandise partners.
How do I use a promo code? ▾
Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. You'll find the promo code field in the Order Summary section. Enter your code and click "Apply" to see the discount.
Do you ship internationally? ▾
Currently we ship within India only. International shipping is coming soon for Australia, England, New Zealand, South Africa, and West Indies.
